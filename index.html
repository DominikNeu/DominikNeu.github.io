... a method to prevent people with epilepsy from death of SUDEP?

The other day I listened to this podcast about a AI that can prevent people with epilepsy from dieing of SUDEP. The AI researcher Rosalind Picard is talking about how she and here team managed to develop an AI that can detect seizures - the technical components are only (in) a wristband. You will definitely ask what is SUDEP. Well you know I'am no expert but overall, SUDEP can happen after a seizure and occurs in the way, that the persons breathing stops. If the person is not stimulated in that period of time also the heart stops.
If you want further information about that, as i find, very interesting topic make sure you check out the website I linked you here or if you are more interested in the technical side, you can just listen to the podcast below.
